About BBQ International

Welcome to BBQ International, where we specialize in bringing you the best barbecue experience possible. Our passion for grilling and smoking meat drives us to provide top-notch products and services to our customers. With a dedication to quality and flavor, we aim to make every barbecue gathering memorable.

Our Story

Founded in 1990 by Premsingh Padya, BBQ International started as a small family-owned business in Texas. Over the years, we've grown into a global brand known for our expertise in all things barbecue. From humble beginnings to becoming a household name, our commitment to excellence remains unwavering.
